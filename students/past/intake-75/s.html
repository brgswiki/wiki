“Do a report”, the teacher said.

How easy, I though, till I got to bed…

I forgot to do it earlier on in the night,

So I am lying here thinking just what to write.

Now how can I start and who can I mention?

Leave out the ones who have been in detention,

If I did that there would be nothing to write,

Because nearly everyone has been kept in at night.
