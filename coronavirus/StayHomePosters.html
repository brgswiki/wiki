A Brilliant Idea

During the beginning of lockdown, in March 2020, Mr Reeves posted a brilliant idea on the "BRGS News" Google Classroom: designing lockdown posters. Now we all know that this was a vital chance to show your image editing skills, but more importantly - how many memes you can fit into one poster.

Some posters

I'm now going to proceed to rate these posters, based on several factors such as:

  • Is it not an eyesore?
  • How much colour does it have?
  • How many memes does it have?
  • How good are the memes?
  • Does it convince me to stay inside?

Big Chungus Poster

The poster conveys a strong strong sense of commandment, and clearly demonstrates how going outside will result in your immediate illness. It gives me the urge to become the piggy on the right, and become that happy and handsome in my life. Additionally, the iconic figure of big chungus and it's insane authority will convince you to stay home, and it's insults will surely convince the rest of the British public to also stay home. Also the NHS “logo” in the bottom right asserts the fact that this is an official and legit poster that should be taken seriously.

  • Not an eyesore - 7/10
  • Colour - 6/10
  • Memes quantity - 8/10
  • Meme quality - 7/10
  • Meaning - 9/10
  • Overall - 7.5/10
