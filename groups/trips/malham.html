Malham

Yet another geography trip, this one to some fields and rocks rather than the buildings down the road. It replaced the trip to the Yorkshire Dales & Whitescar Dales for unknown reasons, presumably because Malham is said to be more impressive but personally I think some damp caves with Halloween decorations from months ago still up and low ceilings for people to bump their heads on is more fun.

Pros

  • Get to watch a number of people get their feet stuck in grykes
  • You get some pretty good toffee
  • Last but not least, you get to miss lessons

Cons

  • They force you to wear a coat with body guard like intentions
  • You spend the next term discussing limestone on the trip and therefore it is debated if the trip is actually worth this struggle.
