Salford Quays

This is a geography trip taken at GCSE, as part of the human geography topic.

When we asked Miss Foster what would happen on this trip she told us it was just getting to look around the Christmas markets. This is a lie. The teachers get to go to the Christmas markets. Students have to go around asking people to fill in surveys and actually socially interact with random strangers - which is arguably harder than the geography GCSE itself.
