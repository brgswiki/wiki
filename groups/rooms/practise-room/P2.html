Practice Room 2

Practice room 1 is a music practice room that is used during music lessons for you to break off into small groups and practice your ‘music’. On Monday afternoon, all day Wednesday and Thursday afternoons, Mrs Spall teaches private Piano and Woodwind Lessons in here. It offers a great spying view over the canopy to see what the dinner ladies are doing at that moment in time. It is also above Room 56, making any unlucky Geography Teacher in there hate anybody using the practice room.
