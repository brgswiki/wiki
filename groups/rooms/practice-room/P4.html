Practice Room 4

Practice room 4 is a music practice room that is used during music lessons for you to break off into small groups and practice your ‘music’. Mr Hoyle teaches private string lessons in here, making it infamous for the awful screeches coming from his sub-grade 1 players. It offers a great spying view over the canopy to see what the dinner ladies are doing at that moment in time. As with most of the practice rooms, it is also above Room 56, making any unlucky Geography Teacher in there hate anybody using the practice room.
