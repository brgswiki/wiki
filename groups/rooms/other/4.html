Room 4

Room 4 is a storage room for the lab technicians. No one actually knows what is in this room although the door has a lot of danger stickers and 'dangerous chemicals' warnings. 

It is more of a cupboard really although BRGS probably just gave it a number so they could boast they had more rooms in the newspaper when the main building was first built. 

It is one of the only science related rooms not to be connected to another.
