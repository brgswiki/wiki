Room 38

This room was mostly used by Mr Fitton as it contains enough computers (at a push) for a technology class, as there are only 20 students per class. It is smaller than the other classrooms in the basement corridor, and if you sit in this room you are bound to have your chair tangled in at least 3 other chair legs, making it a struggle to enter and exit the classroom.

It is no longer in much use as the DT workshop has been since taken over by Mr Grundy and provided with laptops. Captain Grundy has since left but the laptops remain in place.
