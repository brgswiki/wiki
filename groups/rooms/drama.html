There are 2 main drama classrooms, one for KS3 and one for KS4 / KS5 and some lucky Year 9 classes who get Mr Neve rather than Mrs Fowler-Gibbs/Mrs Workman for their drama lessons.

The actual drama classroom in the Clark Building has the full works with stage lights, costume cupboard, curtains, a stage, theatre posters etc. Room 94 however is much less glamorous and has one short of 30 chairs that were probably stolen from a skip, 6 folding tables that stab you in the back when you try to sit down, and a couple of cupboards that may have been made by Year 7s in Design Technology.
