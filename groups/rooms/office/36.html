Room 36

In the past Room 36 has been an office to Miss Gauntlett, Mrs Cassidy, and Mr Seddon. Nowadays, no one is really sure who's office it is as it changes hands so often.

Room 36 is found in the basement corridor and once housed a range of strange and unusual objects. This included a box of tape measures, maps, and a number of other geography related items. Mrs Cassidy once let a couple of students skip her lesson to untangle all the tape measures in this room, which we did a surprisingly good job at considering the tape measures looked like they'd been through a washing machine, a smoothie maker, and possibly a major earthquake.
