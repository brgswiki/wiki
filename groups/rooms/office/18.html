Room 18

This was Mr Reeves' office, like Mr Porteous' office, is rarely seen by students - although is still more widely known than Room 19 as you can see it from the hall & as you are walking out of the main entrance. It is now Mr Johnstone's office, who has only tweaked a bit of the internal décor - this includes adding a ‘meeting in progress’ sign to the door (and not removing it even when he isn't in) and a black cloak hung up on the cupboards, perhaps a subtle hint at Mr Johnstone's crime fighting alter ego.

The Contents

There are many things in this room, both usual and not so usual, this includes:

  • A lot of plastic cups
  • A number of posters and leaflets on the walls, mostly to do with mental health
  • Some (quite nice) antique cupboards, and a secret room hidden next to it (likely a passageway to Mr P's office)
  • Some more plastic cups
  • Boxes labelled 'sherry glasses', these were outside his office on Parents' Evening...
  • A whiteboard, even though there are no classes in here
  • A round table in the middle, supposedly for the Staff of the Round Table Meetings
  • A few more stacks of plastic cups
  • Some jugs of squash & a tea pot
