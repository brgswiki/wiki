Room 18

This is Mr Reeves' office, like Mr Porteous' office, is rarely seen by students - although is still more widely known than Room 19 as you can see it from the hall & as you are walking out of the main entrance.

The Contents

There are many things in this room, both normal and unusual, this includes:

  • A lot of plastic cups
  • A number of posters and leaflets on the walls, mostly to do with mental health
  • Some (very nice if I may say so) antique cupboards, with one part just going really far back for no particular reason
  • Some more plastic cups
  • Boxes labelled 'sherry glasses', these were outside his office on Parents' Evening...
  • A whiteboard, even though there are no classes in here
  • A round table in the middle, supposedly for the Staff of the Round Table Meetings
  • A few more stacks of plastic cups
  • Some jugs of squash & a tea pot
