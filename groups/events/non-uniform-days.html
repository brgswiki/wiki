Non-Uniform Day

A classic scam pulled off by a number of schools where you pay to wear your own clothes. 

There seemed to be a theme in the past to each non uniform day - of which there were a lot - however now it seems to have been reduced to once a term with the only theme being “NO BUMS NO BOOBS NO BELLIES” (© Copyright Mrs Bowdler).

The most worn outfit is just a classic jeans and hoodie - much to Mrs Wharmby's dismay.
