Room 64

Room 64, or also known as the Estate Office, is a little seen room by students.  All that has been seen in here is the 'noo noo' pipe type things, plastic chairs (possibly even older than those in the languages classrooms), and some traffic cones.

MI6

It is mostly used by technicians and the welfare staff as a secret hideout. As many students have never set foot in this room it was debated that it was used as a rural MI6 site.

Our suspicions were confirmed when we checked the map and floorplan of BRGS (on the right) and there is in fact a little label saying 'tunnel access', most likely allowing the agents disguised as welfare staff to travel between BRGS and their headquarters back in London with ease.
