S8

A (now school) bus which runs from Todmorden Bus Station to Waterfoot and vice versa. This bus seems to be late every single night. The route of this bus changes to something more ridiculous every few months, and each year they threaten to cancel the service as no one gets it other than school children and possibly a few old ladies wishing to pop into Tod for their weekly shop of knitting wool and antique goods from the market, although arguably Hebden would be a better place to find these items.

For whatever reason, Rosso hates this bus service, maybe because it strays into the dark side Yorkshire, and gives us buses that look less sturdy than if they were to dig up and use the wooden Trojan horse as a bus.  The bus number ‘8S’ is the thrid varient of this bus in 1 year (we've had 7, 8 and 8S) Also, these buses have been known to break down every day for a week at some point, often in the evening so students don't even get to miss form or period 1.

This bus is a school version of the 8 bus (Todmorden - Burnley), by Rosso.
