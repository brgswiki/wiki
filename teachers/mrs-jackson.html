Mrs Jackson

Mrs Jackson is a food tech  and textiles teacher who supposedly retired at the end of the 2016/17 school year. The school, evidently not able to find another technology teacher of her quality, kept semi asking bringing her back for a year here and there. However, in 2019/2020, she came back full time (though if you ask her, she doesn't seem too happy) and now teaches textiles to Year 7s and food tech to Year 8s & 9s. 

Notably, she says a mix of 'scon' and 'scoan' and didn't even realise until someone asked which way she really though scone was really pronounced - to which she replied that she didn't even know anymore.
