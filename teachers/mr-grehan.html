Mr Grehan

Mr Grehan is a history teacher and is a head of year (9) and married to Mrs Grehan. Him and his wife are, according to him, 'the dream team'. Once compared a whole year to Nazis. He is usually a very happy man but scary when angry (he gets angry if you fight). He is also obsessed with oil on fire.

He is involved with a food bank charity and kindly lets his office be used for the collection point.
