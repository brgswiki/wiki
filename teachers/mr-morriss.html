Mr Morriss

Mr Morriss (pronounced Morris, not Morees or Maurice), is director of sixth form and a biology teacher. He joined BRGS in 1994 and his first impression of the school was 'splendid Victorian architecture'. His interests include playing bass guitar and aerobics. 

The Fashionista
