Mr Foster

Mr Foster is an English teacher who joined the school in the year 2020/2021. Little is yet known about him, except when he reads a chapter which is meant to be frightening, he reads it really intensely, like someone in the room is actually about to die. 

He is currently the only male member of the English department, breaking their long going tradition of at least a number of years. (Excluding Mr Blacoe)
