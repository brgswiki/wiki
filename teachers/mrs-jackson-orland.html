Mrs Jackson-Orland

Mrs Jackson-Orland (most call her Miss Jackson) is a Spanish and French teacher who normally has orangey red hair (although she would specifically like to point out that this is no longer true). 

When she got married, all her classes wanted her husband to take her name because then he would be called Michael Jackson but unfortunately this is not to be.
