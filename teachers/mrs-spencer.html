Mrs Spencer

Mrs Spencer is a biology teacher who no matter your efforts - you could be Attenborough level - will still probably give you below target in your ATLs. She is also wife to none other than Mr Spencer.

Mrs Spencer is also one of the staff cooks at the infamous Year 9 Camp.
