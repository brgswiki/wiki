Miss Parkinson is an external cover teacher who comes in when BRGS is short-staffed and was one of the food technology maternity covers in the 2021 / 22 school year. She is very sassy and be warned, a lot of the things she says are on the cover lesson-plan usually aren't and are just more convenient for her. She is in fact Mrs Williamson's auntie.

She did charity work in Sudan and has stories for every occasion.

She described to one of her classes how she ‘only works in good schools’ and had decided to retire fully at the end of of the 2022 school year.
