Miss Parkinson is an external cover teacher who comes in when BRGS is short-staffed. She is very sassy and be warned, a lot of the things she says are on the cover lesson-plan usually aren't and are just more convenient for her. She is in fact Mrs Williamson's auntie.

Quotes

Student: Can we listen to music?

Miss P: No because I don't have the contract which lets me let you listen to music. If a teacher were to walk in and you're all here with your earphones, they will tell other teachers, and I will never get hired here again. And I won't get any money, and I'm a widow. Such a poor, poor widow. And I wouldn't be able to feed my children, so they'll starve, and even though they don't live with me - they're 22 and 24. That's why you can't listen to music. Do you want my children to starve??

Student: ...Debatable...

Miss Parkinson: Ok, your teacher said to complete the work in silence, and anything you hadn't finished would have to be done for homework.
