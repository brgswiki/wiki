Mrs Baines

Mrs Baines is BRGS's resident school nurse, when she is not in her office, Mrs B is probably on a school trip. Aside from the usual dealing paracetamol, ice-packs, and wet paper towels, Mrs Baines knows all and can often be spotted walking on a mission to aid an ill student before they themselves even know they're under the weather. 

Owner of the legendary chat2nurse email address.
