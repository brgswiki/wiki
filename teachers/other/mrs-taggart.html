Mrs Taggart

Mrs Taggart is the school's business manager, meaning she deals with anything finance-y and to do with corridors. She resides in the business offices near the bottom entrance of school. 

Mrs Taggart also goes on a few trips - including China - where she can deal out expert advice to whichever falling out she has to deal with. 

Her attempts at ‘the woah’ and dabbing are flawless.
