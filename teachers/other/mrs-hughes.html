Mrs Hughes

Mrs H was previously Mr Porteous' PA and now works for Mr Johnstone. What this prestigious job entails of we shall never know, however Mrs Hughes likely knows and does more about the day to day running of the school than all of SLT combined (excluding Mr Reeves of course). Mrs H also goes on a number of school trips and has taken part in fundraisers, including the abseil down the front of BRGS.

Job Description: By A Porteous

  • Acting skills required for taking part in What Does the Fox Say sequels
