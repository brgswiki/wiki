Mr Neve

Mr Neve is Head of the Drama department (it is worth mentioning this department is only made up of 2 staff members including Mr Neve). His lessons are very diverse and often include things like replicating zombie apocalypses whereas Mrs Workman and Mrs Fowler-Gibbs tend to go for more Shakespeare themed topics. Like the other drama teachers he doesn't like having water in his classroom - maybe all drama teachers are like the witch in the Wizard of Oz?
