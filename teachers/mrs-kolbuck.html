Mrs Kolbuck

Mrs Kolbuck is a PE teacher whose hair is often some shade of pink. She is currently the Head of Year 9. Her office is the one next to Mr Porteous' office.

Often wears sunglasses, although why is unknown as here is Rossendale we are rarely blessed with enough sunlight to make out figures a couple of metres away.
