Mr Bretherton

Mr Bretherton first joined BRGS in 2004, before retiring in 2018 but much to students' relief returning part time in 2019. Mr B has also taught at Haslingden High School for a total of 17 years. Over this time he has inspired countless students to study chemistry, from A-Level to beyond. 

We are deeply saddened to announce that Mr Bretherton unfortunately passed away in 2020 after a long battle with leukaemia, and he is sorely missed by students and staff alike.

Chemistry

Mr Bretherton taught physical chemistry to A-Level students (the mathsy topics). Mr Bretherton was a strong believer in the use of ‘sulphur’ rather than ‘sulfur’ and using the correct version would definitely put you in his good books.
