Mr Proudman

Mr Proudman is a private lesson music teacher who teaches Guitar lessons and teaches on Mondays and Fridays all. He is one of the nicest music teachers, he is constantly positive and has a bunch of really helpful exercises to help with Guitar. He often teaches in PR 6.

A typical lesson with Mr Proudman

  • He says hello and you take about 5 minutes taking your instrument out and asking how each other's week has been
  • You spend a good 20 minutes showing him all the pieces you've been practising.
  • The remainder of your allotted time is spent practicing a certain part of a piece, or start a new one
  • He then realises he hasn't heard you play the full piece yet, and makes the next person wait so he can listen to your piece
