English

This subject deals with those very important life skills such as reading poetry, analysing poetry, reading books, analysing books, delivering speeches, and writing gothic stories about decrepit churches.

English is split into two equally boring sections - English Literature and English Language. 

English Literature

This is the side of English that covers all the analysing side of poetry and books and other pieces of writing.

English Language

This is the writing side of English where you have to write your own stories, travel brochures, or newspaper reports.
