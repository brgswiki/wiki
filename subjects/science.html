Science

Science is the combined subject of biology, chemistry, and physics taught to Years 7 and 8. This subject is timetabled 6 times a fortnight, equal with maths and English. 

At the start of Year 7 you cover riveting topics like the human skeleton and the solar system (although a trip to Jodrell Bank may come out of this if you're lucky), in Year 8 you move onto less fun topics such as the human digestive system and food tests.
