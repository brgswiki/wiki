Science

Science is the combined subject of biology, chemistry, and physics taught to Years 7 and 8. This subject is timetabled 6 times a fortnight, equal with maths and English. 

At the start of Year 7 you cover riveting topics like the human skeleton and the solar system (although a trip to Jodrell Bank may come out of this if you're lucky), in Year 8 you move onto the human digestive system and food tests. If you are particularly chemistry focused you may end up being one of two from Year 7 / Year 8 to go to the Salter's Chemistry Competition which takes place in the University of Manchester and means a day off from school to do some fun mixing of chemicals in an attempt to solve crime.
