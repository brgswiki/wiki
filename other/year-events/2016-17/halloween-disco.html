Halloween Disco

One of the last Halloween discos to be held, and welcomed the current Year 11 into a taster of BRGS Events. Of course, it couldn't be a proper BRGS event without the staff's stormtrooper dance which can be seen here (if you manage to recognise your own Year 7 voice - apologies!)

As no one really knew anyone, the main party was in each form room, where each person would sit in their form room chatting and eating crisps whilst Mrs Bowdler enthusiastically tried and failed to get everyone on the dance floor - herself probably having done the most dancing that day out of the whole year.

Each form had their own dress up theme, S - things beginning with S, N - the undead, W - pyjamas etc.
