Head of Year

The Head of Year is a teacher who follows your year group through from Year 7, to Year 11. 

The Head of Years organise fund raising events for their year and are in charge of year assemblies (in which they give out notices and are usually the main speaker, and mostly talk about the ‘inner wolf’ or cyberbullying) as well as school trips.
