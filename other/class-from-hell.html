Class from Hell

A series of videos produced for a year 11 leavers' day many years ago. Mr Wilbraham apparently has these on DVD.

Mr Wyton

This set of videos revolved around Mr Wyton slowly losing more and more items of clothing as it progressed... In the last video where Mr Wyton is supposedly clothingless, a lot of teachers are meant to 'scream in horror' but if you look closely a lot of them look a little too happy...

(Please note you need a school email account to view the videos)

Mr Whyte

This series follows Mr Whyte and shows a true story of character progression. In the first episode, he only has chewing gum and by the end he gets a whole school lunch served to him during class!
