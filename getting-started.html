We won't be harsh on improper English however some of our more serious rules such as being blatantly offensive or disrespecting other people's privacy will be dealt with severely.

  • Previous breaching of the rules will be taken into account if you wish to apply for a higher position such as editor or moderator.

Privacy

  • Please be aware that this website is intended for event and anecdotes about BRGS, specifically at school (or on school trips). It isn't a gossip site and should not be used to post information about peoples' personal lives, both students and teachers. Any information posted that isn't relevant to the school and its occupants, will be removed, and the poster warned. If a staff member has told you a story in lesson (even if it isn't related to BRGS) this is still ok to share, as long as the staff member has told you the story themselves.
  • This rule covers any information about lives, including where someone was born, email addresses, mobile phone numbers and so forth. Adding these details to the site (even if they are your own details!) is a breach of this policy and will result in a reprimand from the moderators/admins.
  • Also, please ensure that rumours about teachers, students etc are NOT posted. We understand that some people may have no issue with certain facts being made public, but as we cannot verify who is behind a username, we cannot possibly know if the person in question has given their consent. This is not Facebook after all, and the site must be kept factual and relevant to school life. If you are unsure what to put, have a go on the old BRGS Wiki 'random page' link - you'll usually come across some good examples of what to put on a student page!
  • As ever, if you have an issue with the content of this site, ask us via DM on Instagram or send an email to us and a Moderator can alter or delete it if necessary.

Hierarchy

Unfortunately, to keep the wiki a safe and positive community we need some kind of hierarchy. Once you have registered an account you should be able to do most things, yet we have moderators and admins in place to keep you safe, and make sure the wiki is free from any vandalism or bullying.
